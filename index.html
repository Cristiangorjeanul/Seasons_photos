PHOTO GUIDE
FOR
ALL SEASONS

SPRING PHOTOS ADVICE

Get perfect photos if you get close to the subject,
if you pick one subject and choose
ISO 200, f/3.6, 16-35mm lens!



Spring
in
Austria


SUMMER PHOTOS ADVICE

Get perfect photos if you choose the period
after sunrise and before sunset and
ISO 100, f/10, 16-50mm lens!



Summer
in
America


AUTUMN PHOTOS ADVICE

Get beautiful multicolored shades of leaves,
if you pop your camera on a tripod and
use 5x zoom ISO 200, f/8, 16-50mm lens!



Autumn
in
Romania


WINTER PHOTOS ADVICE

Get interesting photos when it`s snowing,
if you use filters, fast shutter speeds and
ISO 100, f/16, 16-35mm lens!



Winter
in
Finland


LIGHT FILTERING ADVICE

Filters are recommended to reduce reflections,
the amount of light entering the lens
and the effect of purple fringing!



The filters also
protect the lens!


FLASH ADVICE

Landscape photography can only be done
with natural ambient light!
Artificial light is only recommended for lighting shaded areas!



It is not recommended
to photograph landscapes
in the dark, rain or storm!


NEW IDEA FOR YOUR PORTFOLIO!

Try to photograph the same tree from the same point each season!
